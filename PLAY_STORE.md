### Long Description

```
⚔️ Ottoman Chess — History Lives on the Board ⚔️

Witness the legendary battles of two great Turkish empires on the chessboard! Ottoman Chess transforms the classic game with historically themed piece names, ruler portraits, and real battle scenarios.

🗡️ TWO GREAT HISTORICAL BATTLES

🟢 BATTLE OF ANKARA 1402
Sultan Bayezid faces Emir Timur. Two rulers of Anatolia meet on the board for the fate of a plain. Janissary, Sipahi, Akinci, Vizier, Grand Vizier, and Shah Bayezid represent the Ottoman army. Opposite stand Mirza, Tumen Commander, Bahadir, Grand Vizier, Commander of Commanders, and Timur.

🔴 BATTLE OF ÇALDIRAN 1514
Yavuz Sultan Selim faces Shah Ismail. The era of cavalry closes before cannons and rifles. On the Ottoman side, the same Ottoman pieces; on the Safavid side, Qizilbash, Cavalry Commander, Ezhere, Murshid, Deputy Sultan, and Shah Ismail.

🎮 GAME MODES

🤖 Against AI — 3 difficulty levels
👥 Two players on the same device
🌍 Online — matchmaking or share an invite link
🏆 Winner becomes Lokum, loser becomes Dung (easter egg — only in Çaldıran)

♟️ FULL RULES

Castling, en passant, promotion, check and checkmate, stalemate. All FIDE rules implemented. Pieces move only on your turn; moves that put your own king in check are automatically blocked. AI depth selectable from 2 to 4 plies.

🌐 HOW ONLINE WORKS

Peer-to-peer (P2P) connection — no server, low latency. Create a room and share the 6-character code with your friend, or send a direct invite link (e.g. game.app/?room=ABC123). When the opponent joins, the game starts automatically. Every move syncs instantly; the winner/loser is shown on both sides simultaneously.

📜 HISTORICAL DETAILS

Each battle includes a short history, ruler quotes, and battle story in-game. Piece names, theme colors, and backgrounds are custom-designed per battle: Ottoman green, enemy red (Qizilbash).

🎨 DESIGN

Hand-crafted SVG pieces (each representing a historical figure), animated backgrounds, touch-optimized interface for mobile and tablet. Ottoman palace aesthetic in dark/cold tones, fluid animations, sparkles.

📱 FEATURES

✓ Easy-to-learn, rich Turkish interface
✓ 2 battle scenarios (more coming)
✓ AI with 3 difficulty levels
✓ Online P2P multiplayer
✓ Move history, board flip, statistics
✓ Works on iPhone and Android
✓ Playable offline (AI mode)

🏰 CATEGORY: Strategy / Board Games / Educational

Ideal for chess learners, history enthusiasts, and multiplayer battle lovers. Experience historical battles such as the Battle of Ankara and the Battle of Çaldıran in a fun way. White pieces are Ottoman (green), black pieces are the enemy (red).

⚔️ WINNER BECOMES LOKUM, LOSER BECOMES DUNG 🍬💩

When you checkmate in the Çaldıran battle, the winner turns into delicious lokum (Turkish delight), and the loser turns into dung! Discover our humorous surprise in the game.

Download and conquer the Ottoman board!
```
